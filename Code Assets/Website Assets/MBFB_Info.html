My Best Friend, Bunt is a colorful show about a mute puppet boy named Bunt who goes on adventures with his main friend group. The show consists of five seasons, and is an episodic show mainly focused on Bunt’s normal life with his friends and family as wacky hi-jinks ensue. The show mainly teaches life lessons based on social situations and allows kids to feel as if they were really hanging out with Bunt himself!

The show was created and directed by Rowan Q. Talis, and without his amazing team this show wouldn’t exist!
