<p id="about">At KidswO, founded in 1978, we produce original, educational and entertaining content for the newest generations to come. We give artists and creators the opportunity to become the best version of themselves and help the children we love do the same. Our most popular shows, written and illustrated by our wonderful creators, are My Best Friend, Bunt and Little Mouse Behind the House. KidswO is family centered and we understand the importance of education for all demographics for all ages. In these shows, not only will your children learn about the world, cultures, academics and social skills, but you as parents will learn much of the world your children were born into and how to aid them best as they grow up. We love your children, almost as much as you do!</p>
